Phnom Bakhen is one of the most famous places in Siem Reap to catch the sunset. Juli is a big fan of sunrises & sunsets, so to give her company I went along with her to Phnom Bakhen. The temple is on a small hill (which is why it offers a beautiful view of the sunset) and involves a short trek to the top. When we got there, the place was packed with people. After a long wait, I finally got some place to sit; and saw the sun go down.

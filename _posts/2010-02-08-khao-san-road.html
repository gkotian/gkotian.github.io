Our first stop in Bangkok was Khao San Road. Vinay had told me a lot about this backpacker friendly place packed with tourists, and it sure did not dissapoint. There were people from all over the world milling about all over the street. We found a decent place to stay, and stepped out to explore.

It was evening soon, and Khao San was beginning to wake up. We partied the whole night long, and got back only in the morning.

This was repeated the next night as well.

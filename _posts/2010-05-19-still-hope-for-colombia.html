Matt woke me up at 4 am since he was leaving back home to Canada. I gave him a postcard addressed to him and his brother Colin. We also exchanged reading recommendations. He recommended “Jitterbug Perfume”, while I recommended “Shantaram”. After that we said goodbye, and he left.

Later in the morning, I went to the Colombian embassy where after asking me a few questions, they asked me to submit all the required documents. If all the documents are in order, then I should get a tourist visa to Colombia!!

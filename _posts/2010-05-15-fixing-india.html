Some of the previous volunteers had painted a beautiful large map of the World on one of the walls inside the library. But India had not been drawn correctly. Kashmir had not been shown entirely as a part of India, the north-eastern states were a totally different color, and the Andaman & Nicobar islands weren’t even there. I had wanted to fix all this for a long time, but simply couldn’t remember to do it when I was at the library.

But fortunately on the last day I remembered, found some old unused paint and fixed it once and for all.

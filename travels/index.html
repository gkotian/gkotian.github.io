2010 was the year that changed everything. Against the grain of tradition and expectations, I decided it was my year to see the world.

I started this blog in the nervous months leading up to my departure - when I decided I had to go "come what may."

At the time, taking a gap year to travel was practically unheard of in India. My extended family was convinced I'd lost my mind. They did everything they could to try and dissuade me. I knew they meant well, but I also knew that backing down would leave me with a lifelong regret.

Eventually, when my savings ran out, I had to come back home and find a job again. That meant two things:

  1. my daily commute no longer qualified as "travelling", and
  2. a full-time job left me no time to write.

So, at this point, it seems unlikely that new posts will appear on this page, but as Forrest Gump would say...

For now, dear reader, I leave you with this...

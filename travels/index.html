The year 2010 marked a defining period in my life. It was my year of travel.

I started this blog a few months before I set off on my adventures (when I had made up my mind to do it, come what may).

But it wasn't smooth sailing by any means. Taking a gap year to travel was practically unheard of for someone who grew up in India. When I told them my plans, my entire extended family thought I'd lost it. They did everything they could to try and dissuade me. I knew they meant well, but I also knew that if I backed out now, I'd regret it for the rest of my life.

Eventually, when my savings ran out, I had to come back home and find a job again. That meant two things:

  1. my daily commute no longer qualified as "travelling", and
  2. a full-time job left me no time to write.

So, at this point, it seems unlikely that new posts will appear on this page, but as Forrest Gump would say...

For now, dear reader, I leave you with this...
